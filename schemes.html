Different schemes were introduce for farmers to help in agricultural production

PMKSY

The Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY) is a national program that aims to improve farm productivity and use resources better in India https://pmksy.gov.in/

PMFBY

PMFBY is an insurance scheme for farmers to provide financial support to them in case of any damage or loss to crops due to natural calamities, pests, diseases, etc. It aims to ensure comprehensive risk coverage and minimize the financial burden on farmers. https://pmfby.gov.in/
